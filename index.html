Grid of Nows

A sonic inquiry into the problem of time



*This website is under construction*


The anxieties and uncertainties of contemporary life enable intruders into our own sense of being present. The past, heavy with all that has come to be, casts the future in its shadow. So how do we make the present become again? A Grid of Nows is a research project that asks respondents to submit small sound recordings of themselves engaged in some kind of daily ritual: making tea, brushing a dog, reading a book, typing an email. Feel free to search by keywords through the recordings and to listen to them. If your keyword search doesn't turn anything up, consider submitting an audio recording and description you'd like yourself and others to hear. All submissions are anonymous. Yours will become an actor in an ongoing composition about the problem of time in these times of growing uncertainty. More detailed instructions to come.
